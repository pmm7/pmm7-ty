About Livingston


My name is Philmon Mehari and I am a student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. I was born in Newark NJ but currently live in Livingston NJ. In Livingston it is relativly quiet and not a lot goes on. It is considered one of the wealthiest suburbs in New Jersey and also has one of the best school systems in the county. I believe that this town is a great place to live for anyone that wants to raise a family due to the towns safe nature and spaciousness. There are also all kinds of people that live here. Every race, religon, and belief is accpeted here with no kind of discrimination! Welcome to Livingston!!
